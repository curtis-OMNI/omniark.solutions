Session 1: The Big Picture (Discovery & Vision)

Objective: This session is where we get to know you and your overall business. We'll explore your growth ambitions, operational challenges, and what success truly means for your organization.

Key Discussions:

  • Understanding your core business, mission, and strategic objectives.
  • Identifying overarching pain points and hurdles that impact your overall operations.
  • Clarifying key performance indicators (KPIs) and how success will be measured.

Outcome: A foundational understanding of your business landscape, key objectives, and initial efficiency blockers.

Session 2: Tools & Systems (Discovery & Assessment)

Objective: We'll map out your existing workflows, the technology you're currently using, and how your teams collaborate. This helps us identify strengths and potential bottlenecks.

Key Discussions:

  • Assessing your current systems and tools (e.g., inventory tracking, invoicing).
  • Collecting pain points related to your current systems and applications.
  • Identifying key points of contact within different roles and departments to streamline future communication.

Outcome: A clear picture of your current technology stack and existing system-related challenges.

Session 3: Deep Dive into Processes (Discovery & Detail)

Objective: This session focuses on the granular details of your workflows, how different processes interact, and the dynamics within your teams.

Key Discussions:

  • Documenting your high-level processes and workflows (not detailed SOPs yet, but enough to inform our roadmap).
  • Uncovering what your teams are actually doing day-to-day and mapping out responsibilities.
  • Collecting pain points related to your current processes and steps, identifying opportunities for automation and improvement.
  • Champion Sessions: We'll conduct specific discussions with your identified champions to gather detailed, day-to-day operational insights.

Outcome: A comprehensive understanding of your existing processes and workflows, highlighting areas ripe for optimization.

Session 4: Collaborative Refinement (Discovery & Validation)

Objective: This final discovery session allows us to review, refine, and validate the insights gathered in previous sessions, ensuring accuracy and alignment with your vision.

Key Discussions:

  • Reviewing the consolidated information from previous sessions.
  • Validating findings and clarifying any remaining questions.
  • Collaborating on initial thoughts for potential solutions and strategic directions.

Outcome: A well-rounded and validated understanding of your business, ready for solution development.
